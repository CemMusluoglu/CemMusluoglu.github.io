Cem Musluoglu

Hello! I am a postdoctoral researcher at the STADIUS group of the Electrical Engineering Department of KU Leuven. I am part of the Biomed group working on biomedical data processing, where I develop algorithms and methods for distributed learning over networks, targeted for applications such as EEG or acoustic signal processing. My research interests include distributed machine learning and signal processing, numerical analysis and optimization, and anything related to graphs!

For any discussion or questions, you can reach out to me over social media or at cemates.musluoglu [ at ] esat.kuleuven.be
